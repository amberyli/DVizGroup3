Background

If we look back at 7th May, a huge fire accident happened in London tower block. This accident left two men in hospital. More than 120 firefighters spent about three hours tackling the blaze.

On 3rd May, Platt's Eyot Blaze destroyed two boatyards. 100 firefighters were sent to tackle the blaze.

Another famous case, the Grenfell Tower fire, which happened on 14th June 2017, caused 72 deaths and more than 70 injured.

Aim

In London, you can hear fire engine sirens almost everywhere at any time. London Fire Brigade is the busiest fire and rescue service in the country and one of the largest firefighting and rescue organisations in the world.

At the following visualisations, we are going to use London Fire Brigade Incident Records to take a close look at incidents since January 2009.
